[h1]Inspiration Potions[/h1]

Adds inspiration potions to match vanilla healing and mana potion tiers.

[h2]Features[/h2]

• Adds the Lesser Inspiration Potion, Inspiration Potion, Greater Inspiration Potion, and Super Inspiration Potion. If Calamity is installed, the Supreme Inspiration Potion will also be added (each is available as a decorative potion, crafted like other decorative potions) 

• Adds the Inspiring Reed, a Mana Flower equivalent for inspiration potions 

• Adds a keybind for Quick Inspiration, configurable in Controls 

• Adds the ability to receive inspiration potions from fishing crates and for them to generate in the Dungeon as placed bottles (both configurable in Mod Configuration)

• Adds inspiration potions to Big Mimic loot pools
