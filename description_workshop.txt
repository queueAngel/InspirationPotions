[h1]Inspiration Potions[/h1]

Adds inspiration potions to match vanilla healing and mana potion tiers. Additionally adds the Inspiring Reed (Mana Flower but for inspiration potions) and a keybind for Quick Inspiration.

Requested by Asherian, made by MaxZet, finalized and published by qAngel.
